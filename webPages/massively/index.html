Welcome to 42
The Future of Education

42 is a global computer programming school with a unique, tuition-free model. No teachers. No classes. Just project-based, peer-to-peer learning that prepares students for real-world careers in tech. Founded in Paris, it now has campuses in over 25 countries, including Codam in Amsterdam.

  • Explore 42
Amsterdam, Netherlands

Codam: The Dutch 42

Codam is the 42 campus located in the heart of Amsterdam. Like all 42 schools, it offers a free, peer-driven, project-based programming curriculum. No prior experience is needed—just motivation and logic skills.

Global Reach

42 Network Around the World

From Paris to Tokyo, São Paulo to Seoul, the 42 Network spans more than 50 campuses in over 25 countries. Every location shares the same educational philosophy: open to all, no degree required, and 100% free.

Curriculum

No Teachers, Just Code

The curriculum is structured around real-world projects. Students learn by doing—collaborating, problem-solving, and building full-stack applications from the ground up. Progress is based on peer reviews, not exams.

Piscine

The 42 Entrance Test

The Piscine ("swimming pool" in French) is a month-long coding bootcamp and the only way to get into 42. It’s intense, immersive, and collaborative. No prior coding experience? That’s okay—many students start from scratch.
